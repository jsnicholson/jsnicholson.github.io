"Ginjen Bend"

Part of the 'Taokodzen' series

Description

"Depicting a secluded bend on the Ginjen river, the lifeline of the summer capital. With no marked artist, we are uncertain of how it was obtained or its origins. Its recovery and safekeeping following the burning of the State Palace suggest it was of importance to an official of the pre-common era government."

Original photograph taken by u/FreeTuckerCase
