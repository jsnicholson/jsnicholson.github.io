Portrait 13/03/2018

"Restored Portrait of a Tao Historian"

"Taode Nifusin (translating to Scholar of the Tao) persists as our greatest window into the old Tao Empires. Born into nobility and with great curiosity about his people, Taode (born as Hoido) compiled the largest known record of the Tao people and their history. This portrait was recovered and restored following the burning of the State Palace in 526"
