"Atop the Misty Peaks (c. 430) (Recovered 1840)"

Part of the 'Taokodzen' series

Description

"An artifact recovered from official archives destroyed during the burning of the state palace. We believe it was painted around 430ce, depicting the crown of Buiido’s temple shadowed by the surrounding mountains. Nothing is known about it’s painter, simply ‘a peasant of the area’ as stated by Nifusin when the image was catalogued"
